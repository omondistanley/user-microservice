Phase 6 Runbook (PWA, Offline, Theme, Onboarding, A11y)

=== PWA ===
- manifest: /static/manifest.json (name, start_url, display standalone, theme_color). Add 192/512 icons under /static/icons/ for full install support.
- Service worker: /static/sw.js. Cache version in sw.js (CACHE_VERSION) — bump to invalidate caches on deploy.
- Strategy: cache-first for /static/*, network-first for /api/* GET.
- Register in layout: navigator.serviceWorker.register('/static/sw.js').

=== Offline queue ===
- OfflineQueue.enqueue('expense'|'income', payload) stores in localStorage key expense_offline_queue.
- On window 'online', replayAll() sends queued items with Idempotency-Key = queue item id to avoid duplicates.
- Status per item: pending → synced (removed) or failed (kept, retriable). See static/js/offline_queue.js.

=== Theme ===
- theme.js: reads/writes localStorage key expense_theme (dark|light|system). System follows prefers-color-scheme.
- Body classes: theme-dark, theme-light. Light overrides in main.css (--bg-base, --text-primary, etc.).
- Toggle in nav panel: #theme-select. Apply on change.

=== Onboarding ===
- Banner #onboarding-banner shown when logged in and not dismissed. Dismiss sets expense_onboarding_dismissed in localStorage.
- Empty states: expenses/income/budgets/recurring use .empty-state and contextual CTAs (e.g. "Add your first expense").

=== Accessibility ===
- Skip link: .skip-to-content (focus moves it into view). Target #main-content on main.
- Focus: *:focus-visible outline 2px accent. Ensure modals/dropdowns are keyboard-focusable and trap focus where needed.
- Labels: form inputs have <label for="id"> or aria-label; primary actions have aria-label where icon-only.

=== Cache invalidation ===
Change CACHE_VERSION in frontend/static/sw.js (e.g. v1 → v2) and redeploy so clients fetch new sw and purge old caches.
