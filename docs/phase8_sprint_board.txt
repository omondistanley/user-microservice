Ticket: P8-S1-T3

Status: TODO

Phase 8 Sprint 1 Definition of Done
- CI/CD and operational controls are consistent across services.
- Redis-backed scale modes and worker scheduling are production-ready.
- Metrics, load testing, and backup/restore workflows are fully documented.
- Reliability improvements are measurable and repeatable.
